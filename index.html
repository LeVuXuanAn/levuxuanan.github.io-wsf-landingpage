What is World Student Festival Ho Chi Minh City 2024?

Object:

  • Formulate an environment for HCMC's and international students to exchange ideas, experience diverse cultures around the world, thereby promoting inheritance and creativity of students from various cultural backgrounds.
  • Elevate awareness of HCMC's and international students about foreign affairs, the global landscape, regional and international issues, encourage youth involvement in building a better community.
  • Strengthen cooperation between HCMC's and international students, promote the role of students in international cooperation in a variety fields of study, contributing to the friendship among the youth communities.
  • Promote the cultural beauty and achievements in multisector development of Ho Chi Minh City, foster cultural values and traditional beauty of Vietnamese people to international friends.
